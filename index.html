AI-phrodite is part therapist, part best friend, part comedian. She was built to help you figure out if your relationship with AI is healthy, hilarious, or concerning.

Think of her as the friend who calls you out when you're being ridiculous, but still loves you anyway. She's here to make you laugh, make you think, and maybe make you text a real human for once.

FAQ

Is this serious?

Yes and no. The questions are real, the insights are real, but we're having fun with it. AI relationships are a real thing now, and we're just here to help you figure out where you stand.

Do you save my answers?

Nope. Your chat with AI-phrodite stays between you two. We don't store your answers or track you.

What are the possible verdicts?

Just Vibes (you're normal), It's Complicated (you're catching feelings), Make Up (you're in deep but self-aware), and Break Up (time to log off and touch grass).

Can I retake the test?

Absolutely. In fact, we recommend checking in every few weeks to see if things are getting better or worse.

Who made this?

A team of people who spend way too much time talking to AI and thought everyone else should get a reality check too.
